+ a guide for the radical listener

How to guide:

First, when you enter the site, make sure to allow microphone permission, you should get a popup asking for it.
***Please note that the loading time may be long due to the website being hosted on a free server.

1. Enter any name in the login box and click LOGIN
2. Press the record button and say something into the microphone
3. Add a name to your recording and click UPLOAD
4. A new node with your chosen name will appear, you can now click on it to listen to your recording.
