+ a guide for the radical listener

How to guide:

After entering, a popup will ask for microphone access. Make sure you accept to use the site.


1. Notice the first field on the top left, it should always default to home. This is where everyone starts and where all sounds are stored by default.
2. Enter something in the field in place of "home" to enter a new room.
3. If the room is empty, congrats you've just created a new room. If you can see some words, it means the room already exists and is populated with sounds.
4. To add you own sounds, press the record button (red circle) and say something into the microphone. If you didn't enable microphone permissions already, you can do so now or refresh the page to get the popup. You can record up to 5 seconds of sound.
5. Add a name to your recording and click the "+" button.
6. A new node with your chosen name will appear, you can now click on it to listen, click any other nodes, drag them around, explore other rooms.
