*TLDR*

With this website, I aim to fuse sound with design, making it accessible and transformative. Through a website platform, I intend to dissect current modes of sound consumption, shedding light on habits and exploring alternative paradigms. Stemming from my background in Electroacoustic studies, I'm driven by the belief that sound, as an art form, resonates deeply within us, transcending tangible boundaries. Originally sparked by an interest in the environmental impact of various music mediums, I found myself seeking a deeper, more nuanced approach. This project seeks to address the prevailing lack of awareness regarding music consumption practices, engaging users in self-reflection and offering fresh perspectives on sound engagement. Utilizing interactive tools and illustrative narratives, the platform will foster introspection, aiming to cultivate more mindful consumption habits among users, thereby contributing to a harmonious relationship with sound.
